Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information collected through this website (wdharrison.com).

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from users of our website:


  • IP Address: When you visit our website, we may collect your IP address for purposes of security, analytics, and to improve our services.

Cloudflare Analytics: We use Cloudflare Analytics to gather insights about website traffic and usage. Cloudflare Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing activity. This information helps us improve the performance and security of our website. For more details, please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare Turnstile: For our contact form, we use Cloudflare Turnstile, a CAPTCHA alternative, to prevent automated bots from submitting the form. Turnstile may collect data such as your IP address and browser information to verify that you are human. This data is processed by Cloudflare under their Privacy Policy.

How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:


  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our website.
  • To analyze website traffic and performance using Cloudflare Analytics.
  • To prevent automated bots from submitting the contact form using Cloudflare Turnstile.

Data Protection

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no transmission of data over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data that we have collected.

Contact Us
